Careers at Enchante Tea House

Join our enchanting team and bring joy and whimsy to our guests!

Actor

Immerse our guests in a world of 1800's whimsy. Portraying historical figures or fantastical characters, you'll be at the heart of the Enchante experience. Portfolio required upon request.

Pastry Chef Assistant

Assist in creating delectable pastries that delight our guests. Contribute to our menu of traditional and imaginative treats.

Landscaper

Help maintain the enchanting grounds of the Tea House, ensuring a beautiful and inviting garden experience for all our visitors.

We encourage all interested applicants to submit an application. Be part of our story at Enchante Tea House.
